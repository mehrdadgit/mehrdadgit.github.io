Within Radius Alexa Skill (Beta)

This is the home page for the Alexa Skill Within Radius.

You may test this skill by:

  1. Enabling the skill by navigating to Skill Page.
  2. Installing the Alexa App on Android or iOS
  3. Invoking the skill by asking Alexa phrases like:
    • 'Alexa, ask within radius to find the best cafe in two kilometres.'
    • 'Alexa, ask within radius the best hotel within five kilometres.'
    • 'Alexa, ask within radius to find the cheapest bar within five hundred meters'

Note if you are outside Australia, you may need to change your Amazon Store by following this link.
